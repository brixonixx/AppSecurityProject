How do I create an account?
Go to the registration page, fill in your details, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email—click the link to activate your account.
Can I use Google to login?
Yes! Just click "Sign in with Google" on the login page and follow the prompts.
I forgot my password—how can I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions in the reset email.
I want to change my password. How?
Log in, go to your profile settings, and select "Change Password." Enter your current and new password, then save the changes.
How do I update my profile information or change my email preferences?
Go to your profile settings to update your info. For email preferences, go to the notification settings and adjust as needed.
How do I create a new post?
Navigate to the forum section and click "New Post." Fill in your title and content, then click "Publish" to share with the community.
Can I edit or delete my posts?
Yes, you can edit or delete your own posts by clicking the three dots menu next to your post and selecting the appropriate option.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Click the "Report" button on any post or comment that violates our community guidelines. Our moderators will review it promptly.
How can I become a volunteer?
To become a volunteer, visit the "Volunteer" page on our website. You will find an application form where you can provide your contact information, interests, and availability. After submitting the form, we'll get in touch with you regarding the next steps.
How do I offer help as a volunteer?
Click on the pin on the map and a pop-up will show up. If you would like to offer help, click the "Go To" button in the pop-up.
What types of volunteer opportunities are available?
We offer various volunteer opportunities including event assistance, community outreach, administrative support, and specialized skill-based volunteering. Check our volunteer page for current openings.
How do I sign up for an event?
Click the "Sign Up Now!" button on the event card. You must be logged in to register.
Is there a limit to how many events I can join?
You may join as many events as you wish, subject to availability. Some events may have limited slots.
Do I need an account to join an event?
Yes. You need to be a registered user and logged in to sign up or view your registered events.
Can I register someone else for an event?
No. Each participant must register using their own account to ensure accurate attendance and communication.
Can I cancel my event registration?
Yes, you can cancel your registration from your account dashboard. Please note that some events may have cancellation deadlines.
How do I change my notification preferences?
Go to Settings > Notifications in your account dashboard. You can customize which types of notifications you receive via email or push notifications.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, you can delete your account from Settings > Account Management. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
How do I change my privacy settings?
Navigate to Settings > Privacy to control who can see your profile, posts, and activity. You can also manage data sharing preferences here.
Can I export my data?
Yes, you can request a data export from Settings > Data Management. We'll prepare your data and send you a download link within 48 hours.
